About Cityline Interiors

2024
FOUNDED

A young studio, building on an old promise: do the work properly.

Cityline Interiors is the residential and commercial interior design arm of Opal Homz Private Limited, a Greater Noida-based real estate advisory with deep roots in NCR's residential market. We started Cityline because we kept meeting the same homeowner: someone who'd found the right property, but had no one they trusted to turn it into a home.

That's the gap we close. Our designers, project managers and on-site execution teams work as one unit — across Greater Noida, Noida, Indirapuram, Ghaziabad, Delhi NCR and as far as Hamirpur in Himachal Pradesh — so you never have to chase three different vendors to get one room right.

We're new, and we're proud of it. It means every project still gets the founders' direct attention, every client review gets read personally, and every "dreams into reality" promise on this page is one we intend to keep, project after project.
